School Building Project in Rural India

Green Earth Initiative

Help us build a school for underprivileged children and provide quality education.

Campaign Details

Duration: August 1 - August 31, 2025
Location: Rural India

Carpentry Construction Teaching

We are embarking on an ambitious project to build a school in a remote rural area where access to education is severely limited. Our goal is to provide a safe and conducive learning environment for children who currently have no access to formal schooling.

This campaign seeks dedicated volunteers with skills in carpentry, construction, and teaching. Volunteers will work alongside local communities to construct classrooms, set up basic infrastructure, and conduct initial educational programs.

Goals

  • Construct 3 classrooms
  • Install basic sanitation facilities
  • Launch a basic literacy program

Needed Volunteers: 3 of 5 filled
